Eleven haunting tracks illuminate modern "addictions" as ten demons that people face in their daily lives. This educational musical work uses hard kicks, distorted basses, and cold synthesizers to shed light on addictions from a preventive and educational perspective. Each of the ten songs serves to raise awareness and educate about a specific dependency, which is depicted as a demon. The eleventh and final track is a concluding warning call for prevention, sending a message of hope and healing.

Dark, driving, and cathartic, it always carries a clear message of hope and prevention: you are not alone, professional help is available, and healing is possible. These songs are intended as a prevention tool to create awareness and encourage those affected and their families to seek support.
