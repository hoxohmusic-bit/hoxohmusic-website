To avoid rejections, removals, or suspensions on platforms like Spotify, Apple Music, TIDAL, and others, HOXOH MUSIC creates music that strictly complies with the guidelines. Graphic sexual descriptions involving minors or implying violence; unlabeled explicitness (must be marked as "Explicit"). Content that glorifies sexual exploitation, pedophilia, or non-consensual sex – these will be rejected and not created.

Content that incites hatred against groups based on race, religion, gender, orientation, or disability; implied threats or supremacism. Lyrics that promote hatred or violence against protected groups – we reject and do not produce such commissions.
**Drug Abuse** Direct instructions on drug use ("how to snort cocaine"), glorification of heroin, methamphetamine, or other illegal substances as "cool"; terms like "heroin rush" in a positive way.
