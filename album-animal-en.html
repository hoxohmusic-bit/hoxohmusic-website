Dive into a world where the lines between human and instinct blur. HOXOH — ANIMAL is a musical journey into the darkest corners of the human psyche. This 12-track manifesto from the depths of Industrial Techno and Dark Reggaeton is an unmistakable call to the inner animal that slumbers within us all. Each track is a ritual with heavy bass, distorted beats, and whispering voices that push open the door to hidden desires.

The music moves from the raw energy of "SIN CEREBRO - Solo carne" and the menacing hunt of "LOBO" to the uninhibited ecstasy of "LA FIESTA". The album also explores the dangerous dynamics of power and surrender in tracks like "PAPI" and "TÓXICA". It is a soundtrack for everyone ready to let go of the leash. The album celebrates the dark impulses that drive us and the rituals that make us who we truly are: wild, untamed animals. Are you ready to surrender?
